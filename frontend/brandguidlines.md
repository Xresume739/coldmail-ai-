# 🎨 ColdMail AI – Brand Guidelines

## 🧠 Brand Positioning

ColdMail AI is:
👉 A fast, intelligent tool for writing high-converting cold emails

It is NOT:
❌ A complicated marketing suite
❌ A bulky CRM

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

* Startup founders
* Freelancers
* Students applying for jobs
* Indie hackers

---

## 💬 Brand Voice

### Tone:

* Clear
* Confident
* Slightly casual
* Human (not robotic)

### Avoid:

* Corporate jargon
* Overly salesy language
* AI-sounding text

---

## 🧾 Messaging Style

### Headlines:

* Short and punchy

Examples:

* "Write Emails That Get Replies"
* "Cold Outreach, Done Right"
* "Stop Getting Ignored"

---

### Subtext:

* Simple and benefit-driven

Example:
"Generate personalized cold emails in seconds using AI"

---

## 🎨 Visual Style

### Colors:

* Primary: Black / Dark Gray (#0f0f0f)
* Secondary: White (#ffffff)
* Accent: Blue or Purple (for buttons)

---

### Typography:

* Clean sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, or similar)
* Large headings
* Readable body text

---

### UI Style:

* Minimal
* Clean spacing
* Rounded corners
* Soft shadows

---

## 🔘 Buttons

### Primary CTA:

* "Generate Email"
* "Start Now"

### Style:

* Bold
* Clear contrast
* Slight hover effect

---

## 🧠 UX Philosophy

👉 “Don’t make users think”

* One main action per screen
* No unnecessary choices
* Fast response

---

## ⚡ Product Feel

User should feel:

* Fast
* Smart
* Effortless

---

## 🚫 What to Avoid

* Cluttered UI
* Too many features early
* Complex onboarding
* Over-design

---

## 🏁 Final Principle

👉 Speed > Features
👉 Clarity > Complexity
👉 Output > UI decoration
